Who I am

My name is Dave Lee. I am currently a Senior Creative Technologist at Mullen in downtown Boston.

At Mullen I lead projects across several clients including Jet Blue, Ernst & Young, The Department of Defense and Mass Mutual.

Building interactive experiences should always be a group effort, not a competition.

I work constantly to ensure our team is well equiped to meet any problems head-on with confidence. No matter how busy I am on any given day, I make sure I'm always available to help anyone.

As a Creative Technologist I strive to ensure our sites keep their perfectly designed integrity while also looking just as perfect across all browsers and devices. I work directly with the Design department to ensure we both deliver exactly what our clients are looking for. In any site's I build, the user is the most important part of the experience.

When it comes to solving problems (cross-browser or otherwise), there's not such thing as a blackhole bug to me. If IE7 isn't showing rendering an image, you might not have saved it with the right color profile. If something works great on Chrome for mobile but not on Android's terrrrrrrrible built in web browser, it could be something as simple as a malformed meta tag. The key to solving any problem (Internet-related or not) is learning from all your past mistakes and thinking fast on your feet.

I love using jQuery, CSS and PHP when I build websites.

Have questions about the Internets? Ask my friend, Stack Overflow, I might answer your question!
